    Tips

    Here are some tips to help you stay focused:

    • Try the Pomodoro Technique for time management.
    • Take short breaks every hour to rest your eyes and stretch.
    • Stay hydrated and eat healthy snacks to maintain energy levels.
    • Keep your workspace clean and organized for better productivity.
    • Set realistic goals and prioritize tasks to avoid feeling overwhelmed.
